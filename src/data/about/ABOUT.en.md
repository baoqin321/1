# About

I am Baoqin. This blog is meant to stay quiet, durable, and easy to maintain over time.

I use it as the meeting point between two kinds of work: ideas that are still taking shape, and finished pieces or references that deserve a stable home.

## What I care about

- Software products with clear structure
- Writing, translation, and long-form expression
- Small systems that can be refined repeatedly

## What lives here

- Short essays and project notes
- Summaries around videos or podcast episodes
- External links worth keeping
